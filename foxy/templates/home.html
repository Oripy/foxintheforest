{{ _('Gameplay')}}

{{ _('Playing a trick')}}

{{ _('The first player is chosen randomly. Then for each trick, the winner of the previous trick start the next trick.') }}

{{ _('The first card played in a trick can be any card from the player\'s hand.') }}

{{ _('The other player then must play a card of the same suit. If he can\'t he can play any card.')}}

{{ _('Winning a trick')}}

{{ _('Once both cards have been played, the winner of the trick is determined.')}}

{{ _('If both cards are of the same suit, the player who played the highest card wins the trick.')}}

{{ _('If cards are not of the same suit, the player who played a card from the Decree card suit wins the trick.')}}

{{ _('If no cards from the Decree card have been played, then the player who played first wins the trick.')}}
